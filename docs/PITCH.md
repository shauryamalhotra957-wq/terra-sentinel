# Pitch

## One Line

Terra Sentinel turns disaster chaos into explainable lifeline decisions, resource moves, and public warnings.

## Problem

In a flood, cyclone, heat wave, or blackout, decision-makers receive fragmented reports from sensors, hospitals, volunteers, utilities, and citizens. The hard part is not seeing one more chart. The hard part is deciding what to do first, where, why, and with what evidence.

## Solution

Terra Sentinel gives emergency teams a command center that:

- scores every district
- explains the drivers
- tracks critical lifelines
- allocates scarce resources
- forecasts near-term risk
- drafts public warnings
- exports a briefing packet

## Differentiator

Most dashboards stop at visualization. Terra Sentinel connects visualization to action:

- map to risk
- risk to lifeline deficit
- deficit to resource need
- resource need to allocation
- allocation to public warning and briefing export

## Impact

The same architecture can support municipal disaster cells, campus safety offices, hospital networks, NGOs, and hackathon-scale civic resilience prototypes.

## Ask

Validate the model with disaster-management experts, connect real datasets, and pilot it in a controlled tabletop exercise before any field use.
